SHORT COURSES

The Institute provides continuing education in the field of water and flood management. The Institute welcomes sponsorship of Government and Non-Government organizations in arranging short courses, workshops and training programs.

Such sponsorship enables the respective organizations to train their professionals and to update their knowledge with the latest developments in their fields of interest. The workshops also allow the faculty to interact with the practicing engineers and professionals on field related problems.
